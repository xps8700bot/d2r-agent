# Basin check: "Necromancer can one-hand two-handed weapons" (D2R / Diablo II)

Finding: TheAmazonBasin wiki indicates that **two-handed swords** are a weapon subtype, and that **Barbarian** (not Necromancer) has a special capability to equip them as one-handed.

## Evidence (TheAmazonBasin)

- source_url: https://www.theamazonbasin.com/wiki/index.php/Two-Handed_Sword
- snippet: "Two-handed swords can be equipped as one-handed swords by Barbarian ."

## Notes

- This snippet directly contradicts the broad claim that *Necromancer* can one-hand two-handed weapons.
- If we need a Necromancer-specific restriction page, further Basin digging may be required; but the above establishes the exception is Barbarian.
